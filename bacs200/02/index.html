My parents

My inspiration comes from other people who have done the things I have been aspiring to do and are always striving to improve their lives and the peoples' lives around them. I dont usually become deeply inspired enough to live my life to the fullest by people distant to me, lie celebrities or public figures. I draw deeper inspiration through the people around me.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • they are kind and help others
  • they find ways to help themselves in a healthy manner
  • they strive to improve their lives as well as others
  • they aren't afraid to admit their flaws and work upon them
